Why did you join CA?

It reminded me of my Chinese club back in high school, except ten times larger! I really wanted to meet as many people as possible and CA helped me find a place where I could be myself.

Why did you join board?

As a business major, it's really important for me to learn leadership skills. So I decided to get a headstart and take the initiative to join board. Only a summer has passed and I'm already learning SO much. Who knows what the rest of the year will bring? Looking forward to it!
