Ski Trip

December 10-16, 2016 | Lake Tahoe, Reno, San Francisco

Celebrate the end of the quarter with CAUCI as we kick off winter break at this year's Ski Trip! Ski Trip is our annual, week-long retreat in the snowy city of Lake Tahoe. Join us as we ski and snowboard down the slopes, shop downtown, and cozy up in the luxury cabin. Along the way, we'll also stop by Reno, Nevada and venture into the heart of San Francisco.

Ski Trip 2016 will take place Saturday, December 10th to Friday morning, December 16th. Spots run out fast! Don't miss out on one of the most popular events of the year! Take up the chance to really bond with members of CAUCI and find out why "What happens at Ski Trip, stays at Ski Trip."

Adventure awaits! Keep your eyes and ears open for upcoming news.
